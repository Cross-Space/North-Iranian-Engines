# North-Iranian-Engines
North Iranian Engines is a realism-focused expansion for Kerbal Space Program, introducing a detailed catalog of rocket engines and stages from the Democratic People's Republic of Korea (North Korea) and the Islamic Republic of Iran.

Technical Note: Due to the secretive nature and lack of official documentation regarding these programs, many technical parameters (such as thrust curves, exact ISP, and dry masses) remain "nebulous." These have been modeled based on Open Source Intelligence (OSINT), visual analysis, and performance estimates to provide the most realistic experience possible within the RO/RP-1 ecosystem.

Engines Included:
North Korea: Hwasong-18 series (1st, 2nd, and 3rd stages), Pukguksong-1 & 2, Nodong, Paektusan_1A, 4D10 (Musudan), and KN-02.

Iran: Fattah, Rafe, RLE-4, S21100, Salman, Sejjil, and Zuljanah.
